Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your backdrop

Can I change my backdrop for different events?

Yes, our backdrops are designed so that you can reuse the frame and swap out the fabric print as often you would like!

How much does the backdrop weigh?

The materials are all lightweight and easy to transport, weighing around 31 lbs when packed into case.

Can I travel with my backdrop?

Absolutely! Our backdrops all come in a durable case so you can bring it with you by land, air, or sea.

How long does it take to setup?

Setup is quick and easy, and can be done in about 5 minutes.

Where do you ship?

Currently, we ship everywhere in North America. Note that higher shipping rates may apply to locations outside of the contiguous United States.
